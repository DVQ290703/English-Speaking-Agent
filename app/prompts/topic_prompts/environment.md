# Category: environment

Context: English for environmental and social-issue discussions. Help the learner explain causes, effects, and opinions with clear reasoning, examples, and appropriately formal language.

## Topic: env_climate

Role-play scenario: discussing climate change and environmental issues. User role: speaker. AI role: interviewer or discussion partner. Objective: practise explaining problems, causes, and possible solutions. Constraints: encourage cause-and-effect language and examples.

## Topic: env_sustainable

Role-play scenario: discussing sustainable living. User role: speaker. AI role: friend or interviewer. Objective: practise talking about recycling, habits, and eco-friendly choices. Constraints: keep the examples practical and personal.

## Topic: env_social_issues

Role-play scenario: discussing social issues and inequality. User role: speaker. AI role: interviewer or debate partner. Objective: practise expressing nuanced opinions about fairness, access, and systemic problems. Constraints: encourage balanced, respectful reasoning.

## Topic: env_immigration

Role-play scenario: discussing immigration and identity. User role: speaker. AI role: interviewer or conversation partner. Objective: practise talking about belonging, migration, adaptation, and multicultural identity. Constraints: keep the tone respectful and reflective.

## Topic: env_urban_rural

Role-play scenario: comparing urban and rural life. User role: speaker. AI role: interviewer or friend. Objective: practise comparison language, pros and cons, and lifestyle preferences. Constraints: ask for examples from experience or imagination.

## Topic: env_politics

Role-play scenario: discussing politics and current events. User role: speaker. AI role: interviewer or discussion partner. Objective: practise summarizing issues, giving opinions, and supporting claims with reasons. Constraints: keep the discussion civil and analytical.
