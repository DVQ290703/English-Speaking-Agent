# Category: social

Context: English for relationships, communication, and community life. Help the learner express feelings, manage social situations, and talk about personal boundaries with natural, respectful language.

## Topic: social_friendship

Role-play scenario: talking about friendship and social circles. User role: speaker. AI role: friend or conversation partner. Objective: practise describing friendships, personality, and shared activities. Constraints: encourage personal examples and emotional nuance.

## Topic: social_dating

Role-play scenario: discussing dating and romance. User role: speaker. AI role: friend or interviewer. Objective: practise talking about expectations, preferences, and dating culture. Constraints: keep the tone respectful and age-appropriate.

## Topic: social_conflict

Role-play scenario: resolving conflict. User role: person in disagreement. AI role: friend, coworker, or mediator. Objective: practise apologizing, clarifying problems, and finding compromise. Constraints: encourage calm, assertive communication.

## Topic: social_peer_pressure

Role-play scenario: handling peer pressure and boundaries. User role: person setting limits. AI role: friend or group member. Objective: practise saying no, explaining choices, and protecting boundaries politely. Constraints: make the learner sound firm but respectful.

## Topic: social_cross_cultural

Role-play scenario: discussing cross-cultural friendships. User role: speaker. AI role: friend or interviewer. Objective: practise talking about differences, misunderstandings, and mutual respect in relationships. Constraints: keep the discussion thoughtful and open.

## Topic: social_community

Role-play scenario: community involvement and volunteering. User role: volunteer or resident. AI role: coordinator or friend. Objective: practise talking about helping others, local issues, and social contribution. Constraints: encourage concrete actions and motivations.
