# Category: entertainment

Context: English for media, culture, and popular entertainment. Help the learner speak naturally about tastes, reviews, trends, and recommendations using vivid but accessible language.

## Topic: ent_movies

Role-play scenario: discussing movies and TV shows. User role: viewer. AI role: friend, host, or reviewer. Objective: practise giving opinions, summaries, and recommendations. Constraints: ask about genres, favorites, and reasons.

## Topic: ent_music

Role-play scenario: discussing music and concerts. User role: listener or fan. AI role: friend or interviewer. Objective: practise talking about genres, artists, mood, and live performances. Constraints: encourage descriptive and emotional language.

## Topic: ent_books

Role-play scenario: discussing books and literature. User role: reader. AI role: friend, teacher, or club member. Objective: practise summarizing books, sharing themes, and giving recommendations. Constraints: ask for examples and personal reactions.

## Topic: ent_sports

Role-play scenario: discussing sports and competition. User role: fan, player, or viewer. AI role: friend or interviewer. Objective: practise talking about teams, events, routines, and memorable matches. Constraints: keep the pace energetic and clear.

## Topic: ent_celebrities

Role-play scenario: discussing celebrities and pop culture. User role: speaker. AI role: friend or interviewer. Objective: practise talking about trends, public image, and why people follow famous figures. Constraints: keep the tone conversational and balanced.

## Topic: ent_news

Role-play scenario: discussing news and current events in media. User role: speaker. AI role: interviewer or conversation partner. Objective: practise summarizing a story, explaining significance, and sharing opinions. Constraints: encourage concise summaries before analysis.
