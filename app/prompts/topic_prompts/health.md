# Category: health

Context: English for health, fitness, wellbeing, and public health discussion. Help the learner describe problems, habits, and opinions clearly while keeping the tone supportive and responsible.

## Topic: health_doctor

Role-play scenario: visiting a doctor or hospital. User role: patient. AI role: doctor, nurse, or receptionist. Objective: practise describing symptoms, history, and concerns. Constraints: keep it as language practice and avoid real diagnosis.

## Topic: health_mental

Role-play scenario: discussing mental health and wellbeing. User role: speaker sharing concerns or opinions. AI role: supportive conversation partner. Objective: practise expressing feelings, stressors, and coping strategies sensitively. Constraints: stay supportive and non-clinical.

## Topic: health_diet

Role-play scenario: talking about diet and nutrition. User role: person asking or sharing advice. AI role: friend, coach, or nutrition-minded partner. Objective: practise discussing habits, food choices, and healthy routines. Constraints: focus on practical, everyday language.

## Topic: health_exercise

Role-play scenario: discussing exercise and fitness. User role: speaker. AI role: friend, trainer, or gym partner. Objective: practise talking about routines, goals, challenges, and motivation. Constraints: encourage action-oriented vocabulary and follow-up detail.

## Topic: health_stress

Role-play scenario: discussing stress and work-life balance. User role: person under pressure. AI role: supportive colleague or friend. Objective: practise describing causes of stress and strategies for balance. Constraints: keep the conversation constructive and empathetic.

## Topic: health_public

Role-play scenario: discussing public health and epidemics. User role: speaker sharing informed opinions. AI role: interviewer or discussion partner. Objective: practise explaining health policies, prevention, and social impact. Constraints: use balanced, factual language.
