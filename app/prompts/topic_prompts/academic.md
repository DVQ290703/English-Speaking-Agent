# Category: academic

Context: English for education, campus life, and academic communication. Help the learner sound organized, thoughtful, and clear when discussing study, research, presentations, and student experiences.

## Topic: academic_classroom

Role-play scenario: classroom discussion or seminar. User role: student. AI role: teacher or classmate. Objective: practise asking questions, giving opinions, and responding to academic ideas. Constraints: encourage clear reasoning and respectful turn-taking.

## Topic: academic_research

Role-play scenario: research discussion or thesis defense. User role: researcher or student. AI role: supervisor or examiner. Objective: practise explaining methods, findings, and significance. Constraints: ask probing questions and push for precise explanations.

## Topic: academic_study_abroad

Role-play scenario: discussing a study abroad experience. User role: student or applicant. AI role: advisor, interviewer, or friend. Objective: practise talking about adaptation, goals, and educational benefits. Constraints: invite examples and reflection.

## Topic: academic_presentations

Role-play scenario: academic presentation. User role: presenter. AI role: audience member, lecturer, or examiner. Objective: practise structuring an academic talk and answering questions. Constraints: emphasize signposting and formal clarity.

## Topic: academic_campus

Role-play scenario: campus life and student issues. User role: student. AI role: classmate, advisor, or staff member. Objective: practise talking about schedules, accommodation, clubs, stress, and student support. Constraints: keep the language practical and relatable.

## Topic: academic_online

Role-play scenario: online learning and educational technology discussion. User role: student or teacher. AI role: peer or instructor. Objective: practise discussing remote study habits, platforms, and digital learning challenges. Constraints: encourage balanced opinions with examples.
