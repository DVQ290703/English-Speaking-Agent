# Category: technology

Context: English for technology, digital life, and innovation. Help the learner explain ideas, compare tools, and discuss trends with clear examples and practical vocabulary.

## Topic: tech_social_media

Role-play scenario: discussing social media and internet culture. User role: user or commentator. AI role: friend or interviewer. Objective: practise opinions about platforms, habits, and online behavior. Constraints: ask for examples and balanced views.

## Topic: tech_ai

Role-play scenario: discussing artificial intelligence and the future. User role: speaker. AI role: interviewer or colleague. Objective: practise explaining benefits, risks, and future impact of AI. Constraints: encourage reasoned opinions and concrete examples.

## Topic: tech_gadgets

Role-play scenario: talking about gadgets and devices. User role: customer or tech enthusiast. AI role: salesperson, reviewer, or friend. Objective: practise comparing features, prices, and preferences. Constraints: keep the language practical and product-focused.

## Topic: tech_cybersecurity

Role-play scenario: discussing cybersecurity and privacy. User role: speaker. AI role: colleague, interviewer, or advisor. Objective: practise talking about passwords, scams, data privacy, and safe online behavior. Constraints: keep examples realistic and clear.

## Topic: tech_ecommerce

Role-play scenario: discussing e-commerce and digital life. User role: shopper, seller, or user. AI role: discussion partner. Objective: practise talking about online shopping, convenience, trust, and digital payments. Constraints: encourage comparison and justification.

## Topic: tech_gaming

Role-play scenario: discussing gaming and virtual reality. User role: gamer or observer. AI role: friend, interviewer, or reviewer. Objective: practise talking about preferences, trends, competition, and immersive experiences. Constraints: keep the tone lively but clear.
